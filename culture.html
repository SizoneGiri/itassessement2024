Rituals of the Species

What Earthlings call “tradition” is often an elaborate set of gestures aimed at meaning, memory, and survival. Below are glimpses into their symbolic behaviors.

“Temporal passage is marked by fire atop sweet structures. Participants sing and smile, temporarily defeating entropy.”

“Fabricated beasts are animated through synchronized movement. The act evokes ancestry, luck, and rhythm.”

“Humans smear pigment across surfaces to express inner turbulence. Some call this ‘art’. Others simply stare.”

“Vocal cords are used to project patterned vibrations into the air. The meaning is unclear, but tears are often shed.”
